Campus Life

School Library

established :The Orchid School Baner was established in 2004.

A library is a place free for chlidren and adults to read books. It has many interesting books in it.

School auditorium

Was established in 2004.

Auditorium which is on the ground floor has a seating capacity of around 330, is used for all our concerts, quiz programs, debate, theatre, movie screening, musical programmes etc.

School Computer lab

Was established in 2004.

Junior computer lab on the first floor and senior computer lab on the fourth floor are used for computer theory and practical lessons. The labs can accommodate 35 children at any given time which is also the strength of a class - division wise , so each child has a computer to himself/herself.
